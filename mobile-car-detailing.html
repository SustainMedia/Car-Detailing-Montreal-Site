We Bring the Sparkle to You!

Do you want a super clean car but don't have time to come to our shop? No problem! Nick's Car Detailing Montreal offers Mobile Car Detailing. This means we bring our amazing car cleaning services right to your home or workplace. It's super easy and saves you time!

What is Mobile Car Detailing?

Think of it like a car wash and detailing shop on wheels. We have a special van packed with all the tools and supplies we need to make your car shine inside and out. You tell us where your car is and we come to you. It's that simple!

You don't need to drive to us. You don't need to wait around. We clean your car while you relax at home or keep working at the office.

How Does It Work?

Getting mobile car detailing is as easy as 1-2-3!

  1. Contact Us: Call us or send us a message. Tell us you want mobile detailing.
  2. Tell Us Where & When: Let us know your address and what time works best for you. Also tell us what kind of car you have.
  3. We Come to You: Our detailing team will arrive at your place at the agreed time ready to work their magic!

What Do We Need From You?

To do our best work with mobile detailing we just need a few things:

Access to Your Car:

We need to be able to reach your car easily.

Enough Space:

We need a little bit of space around your car to move and work safely.

Permission (if needed):

If we are coming to your workplace or an apartment complex please make sure it’s okay for us to detail cars there.

We bring our own water and power so you usually don’t have to worry about that! We’ll let you know if we need anything special.

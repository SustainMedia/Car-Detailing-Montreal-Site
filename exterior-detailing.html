WHAT WE CLEAN AND PROTECT

Complete Exterior Detailing Services

Hand Wash and Dry

No scratches from automatic car washes. We hand wash every inch using the two-bucket method. Your paint stays safe while getting perfectly clean.

Salt and Winter Residue Removal

Montreal winters leave white salt stains everywhere. We remove all traces without damaging your paint. Your car will look like spring came early.

Wheel and Tire Deep Clean

Road grime and brake dust make wheels look terrible. We clean every spoke and restore that new tire shine. Your wheels will look like they just came from the dealer.

Paint Decontamination (Clay Bar)

Feel those rough spots on your paint? That's embedded dirt a regular wash can't remove. Our clay treatment makes paint smooth as glass.

Bug and Tar Removal

Highway driving leaves bugs stuck to your front end. Parking near construction gets tar on your paint. We safely remove both without scratching.

Paint Correction (Light Scratches)

Small scratches from Montreal parking lots and car washes. We can make many of them disappear with professional polishing.

Protective Wax or Ceramic Coating

Choose your protection level. Regular wax lasts 2-3 months. Ceramic coating protects for 6-12 months. Both make water bead up and dirt wash off easier.

Trim and Plastic Restoration

Faded black plastic looks old and cheap. We restore the deep black color that makes your car look newer.

Glass Cleaning

Crystal clear windows and mirrors. No streaks, no spots. Perfect visibility for safe Montreal driving.

Frequently Asked Questions
Answering your questions about our plumbing services.

How often should I detail my car's exterior?

Most Montreal drivers do it 3-4 times per year. Once before winter, once after winter, and twice during summer.

Do you work in winter?

Yes, in heated garages or when temperature is above freezing. We'll discuss options when you book.

What if it rains on the day we're scheduled?

We reschedule at no charge, or work in your garage if possible.

Do you bring water and electricity?

We bring our own water. We need access to one electrical outlet.

How long does exterior detailing take?

Basic service takes 1-2 hours. Complete service takes 2-3 hours. Premium package takes 3-4 hours.

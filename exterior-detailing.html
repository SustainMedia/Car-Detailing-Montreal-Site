Make Your Car Shine Like New!

Welcome to Nicks Car Detailing Montreal! This page tells you all about our Exterior Detailing service. This is like a super spa day but just for the outside of your car. It makes your car look amazing clean and shiny.

What is Exterior Car Detailing?

Think about when your car gets dirty. Rain mud dust and even bird poop can make it look dull. Exterior detailing is much more than a quick wash. We carefully clean every part of the outside of your car. We use special tools and soaps to make it sparkle. It’s like giving your car a deep clean and a fresh new look.

Professional Car Detailing Services

Why is Exterior Detailing Good for Your Car?

Keeping the outside of your car clean is very important. Here’s why:

  • Looks Great: A clean shiny car looks much better. You’ll feel proud to drive it!
  • Protects Paint: Dirt and grime can hurt your car's paint over time. Our cleaning helps keep the paint safe. This means your car stays looking good for longer.
  • Helps Keep its Value: When it’s time to sell your car a car that looks good on the outside can be worth more money.
  • Easier to Clean Next Time: Once your car is professionally detailed it can be easier to wash the regular dirt off.

What Do We Do in an Exterior Detail?

At Nicks Car Detailing Montreal, we do many things to make your car’s outside perfect. Here are some of the main steps:

  • Careful Hand Wash: We gently wash your car by hand. This is better than machine washes that can sometimes scratch the paint. We get rid of all the dirt and mud.
  • Cleaning Wheels and Tires: Your wheels and tires pick up a lot of dirt from the road. We use special cleaners to make them look black and shiny again.
  • Bug and Tar Removal: Squashed bugs and sticky tar can be hard to get off. We have special ways to remove them safely without hurting your paint.
  • Clay Bar Treatment (If Needed): Sometimes tiny bits of dirt get stuck on the paint that a normal wash can’t remove. A clay bar is like a special playdough for cars. It gently lifts these bits off making the paint super smooth.
  • Polishing: Polishing is like putting a special lotion on your car’s paint. It makes the paint shiny and can remove tiny scratches. Your car will look bright and new.
  • Waxing or Sealing: After polishing we put a layer of wax or a special sealant on your car. This is like a shield for your paint. It protects it from the sun rain and dirt. It also makes the water bead up and roll right off!
  • Cleaning Windows and Mirrors: We make sure all your outside windows and mirrors are crystal clear so you can see perfectly.
  • Dressing Trim and Plastics: We clean and treat the black plastic parts on your car like bumpers or trim. This makes them look dark and new not faded.

How Often Should You Get an Exterior Detail?

It depends on how you use your car and where you park it. Most people find that getting an exterior detail two to three times a year keeps their car looking great. If your car gets extra dirty often you might want it more.

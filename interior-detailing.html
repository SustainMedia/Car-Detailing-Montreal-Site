KEEP YOUR CAR CLEAN LONGER

Simple Tips from Montreal Detailing Experts

Use Floor Mats

Good mats catch dirt and salt before they reach your carpets. Shake them out weekly.

Clean Spills Fast

The faster you clean a spill, the less likely it becomes a stain. Keep napkins in your car.

Regular Vacuuming

Quick vacuum once a week prevents dirt from grinding into your seats and carpets.

Don't Eat Messy Foods

Save the poutine for when you're not driving. Stick to simple snacks in your car.

Yes, as long as we have access to electricity and enough space to work.

We remove most stains, but some permanent damage can't be fixed. We'll tell you honestly what's possible.

Do you clean car seats for babies?

Yes, but you'll need to remove the car seat first. We can clean the area underneath.

No judgment here. We've seen everything. Extra messy cars might need more time and cost a bit more.

Make Your Car Feel New Inside!

Does the inside of your car look a little messy? Maybe there are crumbs in the seats or sticky spots on the dashboard. Don't worry! At Nick's Car Detailing Montreal we can make the inside of your car super clean and fresh. It will feel like a brand new car again!

What is Interior Car Detailing?

Think of it like a deep clean for the inside of your car. It's much more than a quick vacuum. We get into all the little spots and make sure everything is sparkling. A clean car isn't just nice to look at it's also healthier for you and your family.

Professional Car Detailing Services

Why Get Your Car's Inside Detailed?

  • It Looks Great: A clean car makes you feel good when you're driving.
  • It Smells Fresh: We get rid of old food smells or other yucky odors.
  • It's Healthier: We remove dust dirt and germs that can hide in your car.
  • Keeps Your Car Nice: Taking good care of your car's inside helps it stay in good shape for longer.
  • Better for Selling: If you ever want to sell your car a clean inside helps a lot!

What Do We Clean?

We clean almost everything inside your car! Here are some of the things we do:

Thorough Vacuuming: We vacuum everywhere! This includes your seats carpets floor mats and even the trunk. We use special tools to reach tricky spots like under the seats and in between cushions.

Cleaning Hard Surfaces: We wipe down and clean all the hard parts of your car's inside. This includes:
Dashboard
Center console (where your gear stick and cup holders are)
Door panels
Steering wheel
Glove box

Window Cleaning: We clean the inside of all your windows. This helps you see better when you're driving. No more smudges or streaks!

Seat Cleaning:
Fabric Seats: We can shampoo and deep clean your cloth seats to lift out dirt and stains.
Leather Seats: We use special cleaners and conditioners for leather seats. This keeps them soft and stops them from cracking.

Carpet and Mat Cleaning: Just like your seats your car's carpets and mats can get very dirty. We can shampoo them to remove stains and ground-in dirt.

Door Jambs Cleaned: We even clean the areas you see when you open your car doors.

Spot and Stain Removal: Got a tricky stain? We'll do our best to get it out. Whether it's from spilled coffee juice or something else we have special cleaners that can help.

Odor Elimination (Optional): If your car has a bad smell we can often help get rid of it. We don't just cover it up we try to remove what's causing the smell.

How Long Does It Take?

Cleaning the inside of your car really well takes time. It usually takes a few hours. The exact time depends on how big your car is and how dirty it is. We'll let you know how long we think it will take when you bring your car in.

Keeping Your Car Clean Inside

After we detail your car here are some easy tips to help keep it clean:

  • Try not to eat messy foods in the car.
  • Use floor mats to catch dirt and mud.
  • Shake out your floor mats regularly.
  • Keep a small trash bag in your car.
  • Wipe up spills as soon as they happen.
